Common Questions

How do I access the workout courses?
All workout courses are free. Go to the Courses tab and tap on any workout to get started.

How do I reset my account data?
Go to Settings in the app, then tap "Reset Account Data". Note this cannot be undone.

Where do I track my product orders?
Product orders are processed through Shopify. You'll receive order confirmation and tracking emails directly to your inbox.

I found a bug. How do I report it?
Email us at yamaguchi.dev8@gmail.com with details about what happened, your device model, and iOS version.
